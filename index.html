About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Wendy Perrotti. I am 25 years old and originally from the Bay Area in California. I currently live in the beautfiul state of Montana which I plan on moving out of very soon. I was active duty in the Air Force for 6 years. I’ve been only stationed in Montana but I've been able to travel a lot, especially since I've deployed three times. I now currently am in the Air Force Reserves ready to start the next chapter of my life. When it comes to my occupation in the Air Force, I am a construction worker who's scope consists of a wide range of work. In general, I really enjoy my job. It's a career field I never thought I'd find myself in but have learned to love. My grandpa was a mason, my father was an electrician and my mom an operating engineer, meaning construction is in my blood. But I know this is something I don’t want to do the rest of my life. This is part of my push of just completely college and getting my computer programming degree this month. I have a few hopes of where I would like this to take me specifically into the UX Design field. Getting my degree is also very close to my heart since I am a first generation American and college student. I am also the oldest of 6 siblings and constantly strive to a be a great role model for them. Anyone who knows me personally, knows I adore my siblings as if they were my own. These are just some of the things that make me, me!
